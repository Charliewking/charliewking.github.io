Game Development Information Page

In the spirit of CSE's Professor Emeritus Rick Parent who has compiled a large collection of useful advice for students and links for information on computer animation, below is my collection of information and resources for game design and development. This page is always under revision, so if you come across any broken links or find new sites that you think would be good additions, please email me at boggus@cse.ohio-state.edu.
