Brief Autobiography

I grew up in the midwest and moved to the West Coast after college to work in tech.

It's a pretty simple story. I made a little bit of a splash in college with a project I did with the marching band. In High School I met the love of my life (we're engaged now) and got into jazz. Outside of the that I had a pretty typical life. Whatever that means.


My fiance and I moved to Seattle and we love it.

Who couldn't? It's very green. We don't want to leave. Our cats seem to really like it here too. The opportunities to be nerdy and have others value you for it seem endless. I couldn't ask for anything more. Well.. I wish we went on more hikes, it seems like a waste every weekend we don't, I guess I could ask for that. I blame Netflix.


Interesting projects are my passion. But I can get bored.

Nothing unheard of here. I get into projects and want to see them through. Projects for work are interesting, but I need weekend projects too. I'm learning to not get mired in politics and people. Sometimes it's hard when there are things stopping you from letting the tech shine, but it's part of being a human being.


Last Project: Bonsai Trees; Next Project: Learning R

I started growing two trees. I was told it would teach me patience. The jury is out. In the meantime Caitlin and I are learning the R language. I do like dealing with data and this seems like a good step.
